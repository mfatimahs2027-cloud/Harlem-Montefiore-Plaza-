About Montefiore Square

This community park in Harlem features an open lawn with terraced stone seating, a garden with perennial plants, and a paved plaza that can be used for art installations, markets, festivals, and performances.

The Montefiore Park Neighborhood Association assists NYC Parks in maintaining this sloping triangle. The Association helped advocate for the 2021 park redesign, coordinates gardening, supports community involvement, and organizes events.
